OUR MISSION

The mission of AUBIC is to Foster entrepreneurship and drive economic growth by cultivating a dynamic and collaborative environment for start-ups with sound professional knowledge, integrity of character, a keen sense of social responsibility and a passion for lifelong learning. AUBIC is committed to Empower and nurture innovative start-ups to thrive by providing a supportive ecosystem so that the Pakistan entrepreneur society can grow.
